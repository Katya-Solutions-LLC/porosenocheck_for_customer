# Google Play Account Deletion Instructions

## How to Delete Your Porosenocheck Account

If you wish to delete your Porosenocheck account, you can do so directly from within the application. Please follow these steps:

1. Open the Porosenocheck app on your device
2. Navigate to the Settings screen (usually accessible from the main menu)
3. Look for the "Delete Account" option
4. Confirm your decision to delete your account when prompted
5. Your account and associated data will be permanently removed from our systems

## Data Deletion

When you delete your account:
- All personal information associated with your account will be permanently deleted
- Pet information and medical records will be removed
- Booking history and preferences will be deleted
- Payment information will be removed from our systems

Please note that some information may be retained as required by law or for legitimate business purposes.

## Alternative Method

If you're unable to access the app to delete your account, you can request account deletion by emailing us at anti@rechain.email with the subject line "Account Deletion Request" and including your registered email address or phone number.
