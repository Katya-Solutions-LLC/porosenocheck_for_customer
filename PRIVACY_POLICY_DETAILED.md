# Privacy Policy for Porosenocheck

Last updated: January 24, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Porosenocheck ("we", "our", "us") is an open-source, decentralized solution for monitoring, care, and assistance for domestic and wild animals. This Privacy Policy describes how data is handled within the Porosenocheck ecosystem and related client applications, nodes, and services.

Porosenocheck is designed with **privacy by design**, **data minimization**, and **decentralization** as core principles.

## 2. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to:

* The Porosenocheck customer application
* Self-hosted regional or private PorosenoCheck nodes
* Related open-source repositories under Katya Solutions LLC / REChain Network Solutions

It does **not** apply to third-party services or infrastructure independently deployed by node operators.

## 3. Core Privacy Principles

* **No central server**: Porosenocheck does not rely on a single centralized backend.
* **Local-first**: Data is processed and stored locally or within the node chosen by the user or operator.
* **User control**: Users decide what data to share and with whom.
* **Transparency**: Source code is publicly available for audit and verification.

## 4. Information We Collect

### 4.1 Information You Provide

When you use our services, we may collect the following types of information:

* **Account Information**: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and other contact information.
* **Pet Information**: Information about your pets including names, breeds, ages, medical records, and care preferences.
* **Booking Information**: Details about services you book, including appointment times, service types, and preferences.
* **Payment Information**: Payment details when you make purchases through our app (processed securely by third-party payment processors).
* **Communication Data**: Information when you contact us for support or feedback.

### 4.2 Information We Collect Automatically

* **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
* **Usage Information**: How you interact with our app, including features used, time spent, and preferences.
* **Location Information**: With your permission, we may collect location data to help you find nearby services.
* **Log Data**: Information about how you use our services, including access times, crashes, and performance data.

### 4.3 Information from Third Parties

* **Social Media Accounts**: If you choose to log in using social media credentials, we may receive basic profile information.
* **Service Providers**: Information from partners who help us provide services.

## 5. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

* Provide, maintain, and improve our services
* Process your bookings and transactions
* Communicate with you about your account and services
* Send you updates, newsletters, and promotional materials (with your consent)
* Personalize your experience
* Improve our app functionality and user experience
* Comply with legal obligations
* Protect the security and integrity of our services

## 6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

* **With Service Providers**: We work with third-party vendors to provide services like payment processing, data analysis, and customer support.
* **For Legal Reasons**: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.
* **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.
* **With Your Consent**: We may share information with your explicit permission.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

## 7. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

* Encryption of data in transit and at rest
* Secure access controls and authentication
* Regular security assessments
* Employee training on data protection

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer needed, we securely delete it according to our retention policies.

* **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate information
* **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal information
* **Portability**: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

* **Restriction**: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
